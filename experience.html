Photoshop

I have used Adobe Photoshop since my freshman year of high school. I am very well versed in how it works, and understand the in's and out's of editing on the app.

Illustrator

I started using Adobe Illustrator at the same time as Photoshop, and I am as comfortable with it as I am Photoshop, as well. Illustrator is more for drawing based creations, so it doesnt serve as much of a purpose for photo editing, but it can be used to create special scenes that could be drawn up.

Light Room

I recently taught myself how to use Adobe Light Room. This app is the most helpful in editing automotive photography, as it gives you many more options, and is very easy to change aspects on an image.
